Welcome Words

We, the members of the Junior Council of the KFO5001 Resolve Pain, are pleased to invite you to our Summer School 2024:
Mark the date! 04th-06th of July, 2024.
The KFO5001 is dedicated to investigate molecular mechanisms of pain resolution and why pain chronifies in some patients while it resolves in others. Our projects range from longitudinal clinical studies, characterizing patients suffering from neuropathic pain to animal and cellular models delving into molecular mechanisms behind pain and its resolution. We are happy to look into those subjects as well as life as a scientist with you!
